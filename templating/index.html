About the studio

Atelier is a two-person practice focused on content-first websites. We favour plain HTML, system typography, and build tools small enough to read in an afternoon.

Our clients are publishers, libraries, and small software teams who care about how their words travel — through browsers, through feed readers, and increasingly through machines that read on behalf of a person.
